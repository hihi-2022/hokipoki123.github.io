Identity, Values & Strengths

An achievement I am proud of is may Bachelor of Arts, Majoring in International relations and Minoring in Classics. In high-school, I didn’t do so well. Often my grades were scraping by and I was unhappy, this resulted in me feeling like I wasn’t smart enough or committed enough to achieve things. After a few years of trying different things and do bits and pieces I decided to go to university to do what I was interested in. University was difficult and scary and yet I enjoyed the learning and the environment. I tried to work to a high standard and this paid off, I got good grades in the endless onslaught of essays I was assigned. All of this proved to myself that I could succeed and persist in difficult tasks. This also meant that I goat the magic goatskin parchment of proof of learning.
