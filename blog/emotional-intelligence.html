What is Emotional Intelligence

Eq is emotional intelligence, it represents ones ability to read their own and others emotional states and react appropriately. Eq can also relate to ones ability to stay motivated towards something and resist temptation. Basically it refers to the ability to understand emotions and natural bodily feelings in a logical sense.

How is it different to IQ?

Eq is different to Iq because it relates to a different type of intelligence. Iq can relate to ones ability to demonstrate intellectual feats, it can relate to someones ability to problem solve, their memory, math ability, language and even processing speed. Eq is different as it relates to ones intelligence with regards to managing their emotions as well as others.

Why is it important?

Eq is important in a different way to Iq, it helps to reduce conflict and to relate and empathise with other members of a group. This trait has no doubt been extremely important for our species so far in managing differences. Ultimately it is important as we as a social species must constantly interact with one another and emotional intelligence is key for having these interactions be fulfilling and mutually beneficial.
